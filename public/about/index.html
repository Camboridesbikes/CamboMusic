Me

My name is Felix Cambo. I'm a singer-songwriter, instrumentalist and producer of music. I started my musical journey over fifteen years ago with a little old guitar and a love of making sounds with it.

Later, in my teens I began hopping freight trains and hitch-hiking around the country - mostly enjoying the wide-open expanses of the western U.S.. Busking on street corners and farmers' markets was my preferred method of getting myself fed.

I am taking a leap and am producing my first full album (name yet to be determined). It's such an exciting endeavor and I have so many folks to thank for my desicion and for thier support so far.

If you are curious to find out more, follow this journey and/or connect and support the process, I urge you to hop on over to my Patreon - the home base for this project.
